YouBiYouFang is a game design focused Podcast program, collaberating with NYU Game Center students and Indienoca.com. We are trying to discuss from the game designers' perspective, try to get an genuine understanding in the postmodernism design perspective, and hopefully bring some inspritation for all of us.

游必有方是 indienova 与纽约大学游戏设计项目的中国留学生合作推出的一档游戏播客节目，在这里我们着重从设计的角度讨论游戏，以期在后现代的社会中得出并分享自己的设计理念，并给听众带来启发。
