Smash gear is a CCG table top game focus on the spacial movement. Players need to combine their cards into combo and resemble their warmachines, to destroy their enemies by flipping them.

Smash Gear是一款以空间移动为核心要素的CCG桌面卡牌游戏。玩家需要通过不同的卡牌组合出自己的作战机器，并通过翻转的方式将棋子在棋盘上移动，以此来攻击、消灭敌人。
