Fox Tale is an action game try to push the boundary of Unity HDRP.

Fox Tale 是一款动作类游戏，该作品主要试图尝试Unity新HDRP的特性。

The player can equip multiple weapons to fight in an ancient Chinese settings.

玩家可以装备多种武器，并在古风的场景中进行战斗。

The story is focused on the character finding her memories in both ancient world and modern world.

玩家将多次穿梭于现代与古代之间，并追寻回忆，以寻找自己战斗的目的。
