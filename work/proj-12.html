In the game Monster Town, You play as Devi, the devil lord, who simply wants to have a easy life with his people. But Lai the human princess broke all the peace.

Monster Town之中，玩家将扮演一位恶魔领主 Devi。本来毫无野心、只想带着领民享享清福的 Devi，却仿佛命中注定一样遇到了人类的公主 Lai。

How to deal with attacking human and protect your people, and what's the reason Lai wanted to stay with you?

如何应对来袭的人类，保护自己的领民，神秘的公主Lai又是出于何种理由才要留在这里。一切都在游戏中展现。

The game featured tactics and simulation, you can build work place in your town to tax and summon strong monsters to protect your town, and gradually reveal the stories.

游戏以战棋和经营模拟为主要机制，通过在领地里营造民宿、工作设施来收税，并招募军队。在战棋的战斗中逐渐推进故事剧情。

Independent project, take care of everything by myself.

独立项目，负责美术、程序、系统、数值等一切步骤。
