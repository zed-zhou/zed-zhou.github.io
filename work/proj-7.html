Foreigner is a piece created under the perspective from a foreigner, a young lady who feels somehow alienated.

Foreigner 是一款以非本地人的视角创作的游戏。游戏以一个出身于另外国家的年轻白领女性为视角，阐述她在生活所感受到的异化。

The game uses characters that creates an unrecognizable illusion, which requires the player to read/write a language that they don't understand, to simulate the feeling where the protagonist has.

游戏采用字符画字体，从而衍生出一种「无法辨认」的错觉；玩家需要一边阅读、一边通话，一边解读这些新的「语言」，从而体验到主角本身流转在异国他乡的感受。

Indiependent research project, work on art assets, vfx, UI, code, design, etc.

独立研究项目，负责图像、特效、UI、程序、策划等一切工作。
